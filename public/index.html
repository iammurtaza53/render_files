Can You Get Laser Hair Removal While Pregnant? Here's What Experts Say

When it pertains to maternity and elegance, you likely already learn about the common points commonly thought about off-limits: Topical retinoids, injectables like Botox , and also hydroquinone, among others. Yet what concerning laser hair elimination? The response is more intricate. Why? According to professionals, a great deal more research needs to be done.


" Presently, there isn't enough info to state whether it's safe to undertake laser hair elimination while expecting," says Dr. Stephanie Hack, a board-certified OBGYN as well as Creator of Woman Parts Physician. As a result of that, the professionals we spoke with agreed that it's best for anticipating mothers to plan to reserve any kind of laser hair elimination treatments pre-or-post child.


According to Hack, plus board-certified OBGYN Dr. Rachel Westbay as well as laser hair elimination esthetician Shelley D'Aquino, it's worth diving into the details to recognize the prospective risks laser hair removal presents for pregnant women . Here's what you need to recognize.

What Is Laser Hair Elimination?
" Laser hair elimination is a clinical treatment that uses a focused beam of light, the laser, to eliminate undesirable hair," describes Dr. Rachel Westbay, a board-certified dermatologist at Marmur Medical in New York City. "The light is soaked up by the pigment or melanin in the hair, which transforms the light energy right into heat that harms the hair follicles. This damage hinders or delays future hair growth."


It's worth pointing out that while it's commonly referred to as a "irreversible" type of hair removal, that's a little bit of a misnomer. Originally, several treatments are needed, as well as maintenance therapies may be required, Westbay describes. Pregnancy aside, she clarifies that not every person is a terrific prospect for laser hair elimination, as well as it's most efficient on those with lighter skin as well as darker hair.


The Threats of Laser Hair Removal While Pregnant
There are a few possible troubles at play. Initially, there's the concern of what the laser could do to the unborn child. According to Hack, lasers emit radiation, which can be in the form of light, ultraviolet, or infrared radiation, claims Hack.


She adds that they have varying degrees of threat classes, and also the extra effective the laser, the greater the class. "Powerful lasers can position a risk if misused. Radiation presents a danger to the creating fetus, especially as the body organs are creating in the first trimester. Exposure to high dosages of radiation can result in losing the unborn baby, preterm birth, as well as fetal malformations," she states.


Westbay points out that laser hair removal, in particular, might not be bothersome. "There is no reason to presume that there can be any reasonable damage to an unborn child from the power sources," she says. "Elective laser treatments are generally not advised during pregnancy, but no proof sustains this. The thickness of the pregnant abdominal area, womb, as well as amniotic fluid make it unlikely that significant quantities of laser power would reach the fetus throughout cutaneous laser therapy."


Westbay cites a 2019 testimonial of 22 researches that checked out 380 women in all trimesters of maternity who were treated with different laser wavelengths. There was only one clinically-significant occasion, a situation of premature membrane layer tear with no additional morbidity.1 She describes that the cause wasn't clear; it doubted whether it was related to the laser procedure.


Nevertheless, there are still other potential dangers of lasers that are a lot more specific. "A few of the unfavorable negative effects of laser therapies include shed skin and also hyperpigmentation, which can be more probable to happen in pregnant women ," describes Shelley D'Aquino, proprietor of and head esthetician at Le Parlour NYC Laser Health Facility. Hormone adjustments boost the probability of skin dimming as well as melasma, which raises the potential for these risks, she says. The laser can not tell the distinction in between pigmented hairs and also skin, includes Westbay.


Furthermore, expecting people have actually a jeopardized body immune system, and also any type of burn can take longer to heal, resulting in infection and also possibly also scarring, Westbay notes. Lastly, not all topical anesthetics-- generally utilized throughout a laser hair removal therapy-- are considered safe in pregnancy: "For example, lidocaine and prilocaine are considered pregnancy category B, yet tetracaine is not," Westbay discusses.


Alternate Pregnancy-Safe Hair Elimination Methods
The good news: You have plenty of other alternatives. "There are lots of choices to laser hair removal for doing away with unwanted hair while pregnant. You can wax, thread, tweeze or shave," says D'Aquino.


Westbay adds sugaring to that list, although she keeps in mind avoiding depilatory lotions is most likely much better. "The scientific research is still out as to whether or not they're safe during pregnancy. They contain the energetic ingredients barium sulfide powder and calcium thioglycolate, which cause the hair to liquify right into a jelly-like mass that can be wiped away from the skin. Although no proof shows depilatory lotions hurt developing infants, skipping them is possibly best. Plus, since their smell is so pungent, they can likely worsen any kind of nausea or vomiting in an anticipating mother," she claims.


All-time Low Line

While there's still extremely little data or researches on the risks of laser hair removal during pregnancy, it's never a negative concept to err on the side of care and go with various other hair removal choices up until you supply. That being said, if you do feel strongly that you wish to consider hair removal while expectant, ensure to review the risks with your medical professional beforehand, she adds.
